What This Means: Your Retry Logic Is Costing You
Visa's NVR APPRV REATTMPT DOM US fee ($0.10–0.25 per transaction) fires when a merchant re-submits a card that has already been hard-declined. In a subscription business like yours, failed renewals on churned/cancelled cards are inevitable — but the retry cadence matters enormously.

March's $644 in retry fees suggests approximately 2,500–6,400 unnecessary retries in a single month. BlueSnap's authorization optimizer suppresses retries on definitively declined BINs and implements intelligent retry windows aligned with Visa/MC network rules.
